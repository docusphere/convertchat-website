# ConvertChat Go-To-Market Plan

_Date: 2026-07-08_
_Company: London-based. UK-first, Spain second._

---

## Market Priority

1. **UK first** -- company registered in London, 5.5M SMEs, 73% WhatsApp adoption, English content scales globally
2. **Spain second** -- 91% WhatsApp penetration, Frank speaks Spanish, weaker competition for Spanish-language content
3. **Portugal/Italy later** -- high WhatsApp penetration, similar dynamics

---

## Phase 1: First 50 Customers (This Month)

### 1. Use ConvertChat to sell ConvertChat

The most powerful move. You have a tool that converts email lists into WhatsApp customers. Use it on yourself.

- Buy/build a list of UK small business owners (wholesale distributors, real estate agents, insurance brokers, coaches)
- Import into ConvertChat
- Send email campaign: "You have contacts sitting in a spreadsheet. We turn them into WhatsApp customers."
- When they click the WhatsApp CTA, you're talking to them IN the product experience
- This IS your demo. They just experienced exactly what ConvertChat does for their customers.

**If this doesn't work for you, it won't work for them. If it does -- that's your entire marketing story.**

### 2. LinkedIn outreach (20 DMs/day)

- Search for "Founder" / "Owner" / "Director" in UK
- Filter by industries: wholesale, real estate, insurance, consulting
- 20 personalized DMs per day (not automated, actually personal)
- Message: "Do you have a contact list you haven't been able to convert? I'll turn 50 of them into WhatsApp customers for free."
- The free trial IS the pitch

### 3. Set up the website pricing page

The pricing tiers (Free / $29 / $79 / $149) need to be live so people can see what they're signing up for.

### 4. Record 1 Loom demo video

2-minute screen recording showing: import CSV -> send email -> contact clicks WhatsApp -> they appear in inbox. This video gets reused everywhere (LinkedIn, website, DMs, YouTube).

---

## Phase 2: 50 -> 500 Customers (30-60 Days)

### 5. Write 5 blog posts in English

Target low-competition keywords:

- "how to turn email contacts into WhatsApp customers"
- "WhatsApp Business API for small businesses UK"
- "best alternative to WATI for solo businesses"
- "email to WhatsApp marketing funnel"
- "WhatsApp CRM for one person business"

### 6. YouTube channel

- Turn the Loom demo into a proper YouTube tutorial
- Add: "WhatsApp Business API explained in 5 minutes"
- Add: "I converted 500 email contacts to WhatsApp -- here's how"
- Screen recordings showing ConvertChat in action. These compound over time.

### 7. Join 5-10 online communities

Where your buyers hang out:

- UK small business subreddits (r/smallbusinessuk)
- Indie Hackers (tool credibility)
- Facebook groups for UK small business owners
- LinkedIn groups for wholesale/distribution/real estate
- Be helpful first, pitch second

### 8. Partner outreach to 10 UK marketing agencies

- Offer 20% recurring commission referral program
- One agency partnership = 10-50 customers
- They sell ConvertChat as part of their "digital marketing package"
- Target: digital marketing agencies in London, Manchester, Birmingham

---

## Phase 3: 500+ Customers (60-90 Days)

### 9. Product-led onboarding emails

- Day 1: Welcome + import guide
- Day 3: Send first campaign prompt
- Day 7: Check results + celebrate wins
- Day 14: Upgrade nudge

### 10. Spanish content expansion

- Duplicate blog/YouTube strategy in Spanish
- Frank speaks Spanish, competition is even weaker
- WhatsApp penetration is higher in Spain (91% vs 73%)
- Keywords: "como enviar campanas por WhatsApp", "WhatsApp CRM para autonomos", "alternativa a WATI en espanol"

### 11. Explore UK government SME programs

- Help to Grow: Digital and similar schemes that subsidize software for small businesses
- Research whether ConvertChat qualifies as an approved digital tool

### 12. Meta ads (small budget)

- Only after 10+ paying customers and messaging is validated
- Test Click-to-WhatsApp ads targeting UK small business owners
- GBP 10-20/day to start
- The ad itself demonstrates the product: click -> land in ConvertChat's WhatsApp -> experience the tool

---

## What NOT To Do

- Paid ads before 10 manual customers
- Enterprise sales / long sales cycles
- Building features nobody has asked for
- Expanding to 5 countries at once
- Hiring a marketing person before GBP 5K MRR
- Outbound sales team at $29/mo ARPU (unit economics don't work)
