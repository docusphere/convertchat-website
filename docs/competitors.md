# Competitor Research

## ConvertChats (convertchats.com)

**Discovered:** 2026-06-28
**Category:** Grey-area bulk WhatsApp/Telegram messaging platform

### What They Do

- High-volume WhatsApp and Telegram broadcast/outreach
- Targets crypto, fintech, iGaming, gambling, Web3, real estate
- "Verified Delivery Infrastructure" — focuses on deliverability at scale

### Features

- WhatsApp + Telegram broadcast
- Active-only number validation (pre-scan lead lists for inactive numbers)
- Automated failover rotation when sending numbers get blocked
- Time-zone-aware delivery optimization
- Randomized sending intervals (mimic human behavior)
- AI-driven message personalization
- "Smart content variation" to avoid platform filters
- "Invisible characters" for content review algorithm bypass
- CRM / contact management
- Delivered vs Read analytics
- Attribution tracking per "tunnel instance"

### Why NOT to Copy Their Approach

- Uses tactics that violate Meta's ToS (invisible characters, filter evasion)
- Expects and plans for number bans (automated failover = they know they'll get blocked)
- Targets high-risk verticals that Meta actively polices
- Not using official WhatsApp Business API templates
- This approach leads to permanent WABA bans

### What IS Worth Learning

- Pre-send number validation (check if number is active on WhatsApp before sending)
- Delivered vs Read analytics dashboard design
- Time-zone aware delivery scheduling
- Per-campaign attribution tracking

## ManyChat

**Category:** Multi-channel chatbot automation platform

### What They Do

- No-code platform for automating conversations on Instagram, Messenger, WhatsApp, TikTok, SMS, Email
- Visual drag-and-drop flow builder
- Auto-reply to comments and DMs

### Pricing

- Essential: $14/mo
- Pro: $29/mo
- Business: $69/mo
- AI add-on: $29/mo extra
- WhatsApp conversations: $0.02-0.08 each on top of subscription
- First 1,000 WhatsApp conversations/month free

### Strengths

- Multi-channel (Instagram, Messenger, WhatsApp, TikTok)
- Strong Instagram automation (comment triggers, story replies)
- Large user base and community
- Visual flow builder

### Weaknesses vs ConvertChat

- No email-to-WhatsApp conversion funnel
- AI is a paid add-on ($29/mo extra), not core
- Flow builder is still technical for non-technical users
- Primarily Instagram/Messenger focused, WhatsApp is secondary
- Real costs add up fast ($100-1,000+/mo with add-ons and messaging fees)
- Not built for audience growth from scratch
