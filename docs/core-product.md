# ConvertChat Core Product

_Date: 2026-07-13_

---

## One-Liner

**Turn your contacts into a WhatsApp audience you can message anytime.**

---

## The Core Product (What You Charge For)

1. **Import contacts** -- CSV, spreadsheet, whatever they have. Clean it, organize it, segment it.
2. **Grow the WhatsApp list** -- Email campaigns with WhatsApp CTA button. Contact clicks -> they're now a WhatsApp subscriber with consent.
3. **Send WhatsApp broadcasts** -- New product? Send it. Promotion? Send it. Weekly update? Send it. 98% open rates.
4. **Inbox** -- When people reply, you see it all in one place. Respond, tag, manage.

Four things. No AI. No complex flows. No chatbot builder.

---

## Why It's Valuable

A wholesale distributor has 800 email contacts. He sends an email with a WhatsApp button. 50 people click. Now he has 50 WhatsApp subscribers he can message every week when new stock arrives. 98% of them read it. Compare that to email where 80% never even see it.

That's worth $29/mo. Every month. Because he keeps sending, keeps growing the list, and keeps selling.

---

## What It Costs to Run

Almost nothing:

- **Brevo** sends the emails (free tier: 300/day)
- **Meta** charges per WhatsApp message (pass-through to customer, not our cost)
- **Supabase** stores the contacts and conversations (already have this)
- **Cloudflare Workers** handles the backend (already have this)

No AI API costs. No expensive infrastructure. Margins are 90%+ on a $29/mo subscription.

## Where AI Fits (Later, as Premium)

AI is the upsell for customers who are already paying and want more:

That's a Stage 3 problem. Stage 1 is proving that people will pay $29/mo to grow and message a WhatsApp audience.
