# ConvertChat Pricing

_Date: 2026-07-13_

---

## Principles

1. **No seat minimums** -- directly attacks Rasayel's 5-seat minimum
2. **Free tier that's actually useful** -- gets people in the door
3. **Scales with value delivered**, not arbitrary seat counts
4. **WhatsApp message costs are pass-through** -- Meta charges, no markup
5. **No contracts** -- cancel anytime (attacks Superchat's 12-month lock-in)
6. **All-in pricing** -- no hidden add-ons (attacks Superchat's add-on creep)

## Why These Numbers

- **Free at $0** -- Lets people import contacts, send email campaigns, see the WA opt-in magic. They get hooked when their first contact clicks the WhatsApp button.
- **Starter at $29** -- Below every competitor. Rasayel starts at $150. WATI at $49. Superchat at EUR 79 + add-ons. This is the solo operator tier.
- **Growth at $79** -- When it's working and they want more volume. Comparable to Respond.io's entry price but with email campaigns included.
- **Pro at $149** -- Still cheaper than Rasayel's 5-seat minimum and Superchat's Professional (EUR 149 + add-ons). For growing businesses. AI agent available as $49/mo add-on.

## AI Agent Pricing (Add-On, Not Core)

| | Details |
|--|---------|
| **Price** | $49/mo (available on Pro plan, or as standalone add-on on Growth) |
| **What it does** | Responds to incoming WhatsApp messages, qualifies leads, answers FAQ, hands off hot leads |
| **Why add-on** | AI API costs are $0.01-0.10+ per conversation. At $29/mo, margins don't work. At $49/mo add-on, sustainable. |
